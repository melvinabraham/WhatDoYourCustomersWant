Observations from reviews


Using a Stacked Bar Chart, we have compared the top 8 features mentioned by customers for a particular year. We also added an year wise slider, so that the user can get insight into how the importance of these features changed over the years. In addition to this, the mentions are divided into the following sub-categories:
  • Positive and Neutral Mentions: The positive mentions of the product are highlighted by the colors speciﬁed in the legend. The diﬀerent colors are helpful in identifying the features that are prone to change over a period of time.
  • Negative Mentions: The negative mentions of the product are highlighted by red color. We chose red color to ensure clarity and allow the user to diﬀerentiate it from positive and neutral mentions respectively.
Conclusions:
    Battery remains the most talked about feature across all the years, with 26000+ mentions.
    We see a large spike in mentinons about "camera" and "screen", from 2008 onwards, which is explained by the boom of iPhones and Android touch screen phones. Source
